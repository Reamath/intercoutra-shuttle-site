Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information, in line with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

What We Collect

When you make a booking, we collect your name, contact number, email address, travel dates, and — where relevant — an emergency contact name and number. This information is collected directly from you when you book, whether through our website or WhatsApp.

Why We Collect It

Your information is used only to arrange, confirm and manage your trip — including seat allocation, driver assignment, pickup coordination, and sending you booking confirmations and trip reminders over WhatsApp. Your emergency contact is used only if needed during your trip.

Marketing Communication

We will only send you promotional messages, discount codes, or marketing content if you've separately opted in to receive them. You can withdraw this consent at any time by replying "STOP" on WhatsApp or contacting us directly.

How We Protect It

Your information is stored securely and only accessed by staff who need it to manage your booking. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or request that it be deleted, subject to any legal or operational requirements we may have to retain certain records (such as booking history for dispute resolution).
