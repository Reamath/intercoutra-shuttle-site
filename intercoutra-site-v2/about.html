Our Story

Family-Run, Built on Trust

Intercoutra was founded in 2019 — a family-owned and family-run business built on a simple idea: shuttle travel people could actually rely on. Our flagship service is the daily cross-border shuttle between Eswatini and Johannesburg, and our airport transfer service also reaches further afield on request — including Cape Town International and King Shaka International.

Every relationship starts the same way — an open door. We treat the people we drive as partners, not passengers, and our team of professional drivers carries that same standard into every trip, every day.

Our Flagship Service

Shared Shuttle, Elevated

A shared shuttle doesn't have to feel like a shared shuttle. Ours is built around the details that make a shared trip feel like a personal one — a named driver confirmed before you travel, a reserved seat waiting exactly when you arrive, and a fixed, dependable schedule you can actually plan your day around.

Every seat comes with chilled water, a snack for the road, and wet wipes waiting before you even sit down. It's the same professional standard as our private charters — shared, quite literally, at a fraction of the cost.
